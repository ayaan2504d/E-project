Our Story


Founded in 2012, Noir Essence Paris–Milan was created by two passionate perfumers
— Élodie Moreau from Paris and Luca Bianchi from Milan.
Their vision was to blend French sophistication with Italian artistry,
crafting fragrances that embody elegance, mystery, and emotion.

Our Philosophy

At Noir Essence, perfume is not just a scent — it’s a form of art.
Each fragrance is designed to capture moments, moods, and memories.
We believe that luxury lies in authenticity,
which is why every creation is carefully handcrafted in small batches using the finest,
ethically sourced ingredients from around the world.

Craftsmanship & Ingredients

From rose de Grasse and Sicilian bergamot to amber oud and Madagascar vanilla,
our perfumers work with rare natural elements to create timeless compositions.
Every bottle is a fusion of Parisian finesse and Milanese creativity.

Sustainability Commitment

We are committed to creating beauty responsibly.
Our perfumes are cruelty-free, vegan-friendly,
and presented in eco-conscious packaging. Noir Essence believes that true luxury should respect both the skin and the planet.
