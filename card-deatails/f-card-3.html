    Fragrance Description:

    Tom Ford Lost Cherry opens with an explosion of ripe cherries and almond liqueur, giving a rich and intoxicating sweetness. The heart reveals floral and fruity undertones, while the base adds creamy vanilla, warm wood, and balsamic notes. It’s luxurious, sensual, and indulgent — evoking the scent of dark cherries dipped in syrup and vanilla.
